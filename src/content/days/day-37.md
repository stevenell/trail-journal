Up early & packed - bag weight @ 41 pounds! 🫪

Road walk for ~ 2 miles out of Agua Dolce.

Climbed about 3,000+ feet today - up golden grassy hills spotted with wild flowers and views of surrounding valley and mountains.

VERY hot and sunny again today - not much shade to be found.

Ran into Kaleidescope again on trail - she's slack packing. Met some new hikers today as well.

When we reached our first peak we walked through oak groves and shady paths. Found delicious water from a spring - shirt soaking as well to cool off a bit.

Dropped down to Bouquet Canyon, crossed the road to find an oasis of sorts... Shade, camp chairs, water jugs, charging bricks, other hikers.

We ate lunch and chatted - some left - we stayed and charged up our phones and bodies. Took a siesta for a few hours.

Filtered water and soaked shirts again before our afternoon climb up and over the next summit.

Can see the Bouquet Reservoir in the distance - looks like a dream.

Made it to our planned campsite - one of two spots was taken - we don't have the energy to keep hiking for another spot (could be miles) so we crammed into the other VERY slanted spot - not great.

Cooked dinner on a slant - needed to keep a hand on my stove the entire time - it gets hot and I need to use my shirt as an oven mit... my oven mit slipped and I melted the sleeve of my shirt. Devon graciously finished cooking my meal for me. ...one of many reasons I like to cold soak...

Hope I can get some sleep on a slant - I fear I may roll off my sleeping pad.
